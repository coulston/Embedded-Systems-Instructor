# Ordering Process for Daughter Boards
![Image of Embedded Development Daughter Board](./img/daughterBoard.jpg)

This file outlines a procedure to order development boards from [JLCPCB](https://www.jlcpcb.com/).  
>[!NOTE] 
>These instructions referece manufacturer web pages that are not under my control.  As a 
result the web interfaces may be modified changing the look and feel of the interface.  In this
event, you will need to adapt the instructions to the current interface.

## Order from JLCPCB

1. Download [daughterBoardGerber.zip](https://github.com/coulston/Embedded-Systems-Instructor/blob/main/manufacture/daughterBoard%20BOM.xlsx) to your local drive
3. Sign In to [JLCPCB](https://www.jlcpcb.com/).
4. Click on the white Order Now button at the top of the page
5. Click on the blue Add gerber files button
6. Navigate to daughterBoardGerber.zip you downloaded
7. Leave all the defaults along with the exception:
   - Quantity: Whatever you need
   - PCB Color: Blue has been the standard color
8. Click on the blue Add to Cart button
9. Click on the shopping cart icon at the top of the screen
10. Click the blue Secure Checkout button and proceed with payment and shipping.
11. You should hear back from the JLCPCB in 24 hours.
